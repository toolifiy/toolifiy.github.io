About PDF Watermark Tool

Protect your documents from unauthorized use with Toolifiy's Watermark tool. Add any custom text — like "CONFIDENTIAL" or "DRAFT" — diagonally across every page of your PDF with a professional semi-transparent overlay. Works entirely in your browser with no file uploads.

How to Add a Watermark to PDF

Upload your PDF or image file using the drop zone above. Once selected, type your desired watermark text in the input field that appears (e.g., "CONFIDENTIAL", "DRAFT", or your company name). Click "Add Watermark" and your protected file will be downloaded instantly.
