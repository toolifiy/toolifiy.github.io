About Toolifiy's PDF Watermark Tool

In an era where digital content can be effortlessly copied and distributed, protecting your intellectual property is more crucial than ever. Whether you are an author distributing early drafts of a manuscript, a business professional sharing confidential financial reports, or a photographer sending proofs to a client, adding a prominent watermark is the most effective deterrent against unauthorized use. Toolifiy's PDF Watermark tool allows you to stamp customized text across every single page of your PDF documents or image files. We apply a professional, semi-transparent overlay that clearly marks the document's status without completely obscuring the underlying content. Because this tool runs entirely on your local machine via advanced browser APIs, you can confidently watermark your most sensitive files without ever exposing them to external cloud servers.

How to Add a Watermark to a PDF

Protecting your documents takes only seconds. Follow these simple steps to apply your custom watermark:

  1. Upload the File: Drag and drop your target PDF or Image (JPG, PNG) into the designated upload zone. Our system seamlessly handles both formats.
  2. Enter Your Text: Once the file is loaded, a text input field will appear. Type the exact phrase you want to use as your watermark. Popular choices include "CONFIDENTIAL", "DRAFT", "DO NOT COPY", "SAMPLE", or the name of your company.
  3. Apply Watermark: Click the "Add Watermark" button. Our client-side script will instantly calculate the dimensions of each page and stamp your chosen text diagonally across the center in a bold, semi-transparent gray font.
  4. Save Your Document: Upon completion, a success screen will provide a download link. Click it to save the newly protected file directly to your device's hard drive.

Why Protect Your Documents Locally?

When you are dealing with files that require watermarking—such as proprietary blueprints, pre-release financial data, or legal contracts—these files are inherently sensitive. Your files never leave your computer. The watermarking algorithms run locally in your browser's memory, ensuring absolute data privacy.

Furthermore, this local execution means that adding a watermark is virtually instantaneous.
