Creating a PDF from text has never been simpler. Follow these easy instructions to get your document ready in seconds:

  2. Input Your Text: Type directly into the large text area, or simply copy and paste your content from Notepad, Word, or any other application. The editor is designed to handle thousands of words effortlessly.
  3. Generate PDF: Click the "Generate PDF" button below the text area. Our JavaScript engine will immediately begin calculating line breaks and pagination based on a standard A4 page size with appropriate margins.
  4. Download Your Document: Within moments, your newly created PDF will be ready. You can download it directly to your device and share it with colleagues, teachers, or friends, confident that the formatting will appear exactly the same on any screen.
