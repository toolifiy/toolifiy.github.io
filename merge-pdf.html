About Toolifiy

Toolifiy is a premium, all-in-one suite of PDF utilities designed to give you maximum control over your documents without ever compromising your privacy. Unlike other platforms, Toolifiy processes all your files directly within your web browser. This means your sensitive documents are never uploaded to any external servers. Whether you are merging files for work, converting images for an assignment, or protecting your intellectual property with watermarks, Toolifiy delivers blazing fast results entirely on the client-side.

How to Use Our Tools

Convert Images to PDF

Converting your images to a professional PDF document is incredibly easy. Simply click on the "Image to PDF" tab, and drag-and-drop your JPG or PNG files into the designated area. You can select multiple images at once. Once your images appear in the preview box, click the "Generate PDF" button, and your high-quality PDF will be instantly downloaded to your device.

Text to PDF Converter

Need to create a PDF from plain text? Choose the "Text to PDF" option from the menu. You can add a document title and paste or type your content into the large text area. With a single click, Toolifiy automatically formats your text into a standard A4-sized PDF document with proper margins and pagination.

Combine PDF

Managing multiple PDF files can be chaotic. Our "Combine PDF" tool allows you to select several individual PDF files and merge them into one seamless document. Just upload your files, review the selection in the preview box, and hit merge. The tool operates at lightning speed, ensuring you get your combined file instantly.

PDF to Image

Extracting pages from a PDF has never been simpler. Use our "PDF to Image" tool to convert every page of your PDF into high-resolution JPG images. Upload your PDF, click convert, and our browser-based engine will automatically save each page as a separate, crisp image file right to your downloads folder.

Add Watermark

Protect your documents from unauthorized use with the "Add Watermark" feature. Upload your PDF, type your desired watermark text (such as "CONFIDENTIAL" or "DRAFT"), and generate the file. Toolifiy will automatically stamp your custom text diagonally across every page of the document with professional transparency.
