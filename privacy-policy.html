Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our highest priority at Toolifiy. We proudly operate on a 100% client-side architecture. This means that all file processing—whether it's converting images, merging PDFs, or adding watermarks—happens entirely within the memory of your own browser. We do not have servers that collect, store, or view your documents. Once you close the tab, your data is completely gone. You can trust Toolifiy with your most sensitive and confidential files.
