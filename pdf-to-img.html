About Toolifiy's PDF to Image Converter

PDFs are excellent for preserving document layouts, but they aren't always the most convenient format for sharing content on social media, embedding in presentations, or viewing on devices without a dedicated PDF reader. When you need to turn a multi-page document into easily shareable pictures, Toolifiy's PDF to Image converter is the perfect tool for the job. Our advanced browser-based utility seamlessly extracts every single page of your PDF and converts it into a high-resolution, pixel-perfect JPG image. Whether you are a designer extracting vector layouts, a marketer sharing flyer drafts, or a student saving presentation slides, our tool ensures your images retain absolute clarity. Best of all, because we process everything locally on your device, your private documents are never exposed to third-party cloud servers.

How to Convert PDF to Images

Extracting images from your PDF takes just a few clicks. Here is how you can do it securely:

  1. Upload Your PDF: Click the designated drop zone area or drag and drop your PDF file directly into the box. The tool is optimized to handle PDFs containing dozens or even hundreds of pages.
  2. Verify the File: Once uploaded, the file name and an icon will appear in the preview area. Ensure you have selected the correct document. If not, simply click the 'X' to remove it and try again.
  3. Initiate Conversion: Click the "Convert to Images" button. Behind the scenes, our tool uses Mozilla's powerful PDF.js library to render each page on a high-definition HTML5 canvas before saving it as a JPG file.
  4. Download Your Images: Once processing is complete, you can download the images. The tool will automatically download each page as a separate, sequentially numbered JPG file directly to your computer or mobile device.
